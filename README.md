# 📊 Banking Analytics Project

**Tools:** Excel | Power BI | Tableau | Data Cleaning | Visualization

This project involves analysis of banking data to understand **credit/debit patterns, loan distribution, customer demographics, recovery performance, and financial insights**. Two dashboards were developed to present insights from two different datasets.

---

## 🔹 1. Credit & Debit Financial Dashboard (Excel)

### 🎯 Objective

Build an interactive Excel dashboard to analyze debit and credit transactions, track financial performance, and generate business insights through dynamic reporting.

### 📊 Key Metrics

* Total Transactions
* Total Debit Amount
* Total Credit Amount
* Net Balance
* Average Transaction Amount
* Branch-wise Performance

### 📈 Visualizations Used

* KPI Cards
* Line Chart (Transaction Trend)
* Pie Chart (Debit vs Credit Split)
* Bar/Column Charts
* Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
* Slicers for Interactive Filtering

### 🛠️ Tools

Excel, Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers, Conditional Formatting, Dashboard Design.

---

## 🔹 2. Bank Loan Analysis Dashboard (Tableau)

**Objective:**
Analyze loan dataset to identify repayment behaviour, customer segment patterns, and branch performance.

**Key Steps:**

* Cleaned and structured bank loan data in Excel
* Designed dashboard in Tableau for business insights

**KPIs & Metrics:**

* Total Loan Amount
* Total Repayment
* Interest Earned
* Recovery Rate
* Age & State-wise Loan Distribution

**Visuals Used:**
Maps | Bar Charts | Trend Graphs | Demographic Distribution | Filters

---

## 🔍 Insights Delivered

* Clear view of **cash inflow vs outflow** and spending behaviour
* Identification of **high-value transactions** and monthly patterns
* Understanding of **loan performance by age, location, branch & term**
* Insights on **recovery rate, interest earnings & risk areas**
* Decision support for **financial planning and customer segmentation**
